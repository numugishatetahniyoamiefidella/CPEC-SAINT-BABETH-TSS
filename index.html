About Us

Who We Are

CPEC SAINT BABETH is a private educational institution in Byumba committed to providing quality general and technical education. We believe every student deserves access to modern learning that opens doors to opportunity.

Our dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and practical workshops create an environment where students thrive — academically and technically.

  • Qualified and passionate teaching staff
  • Modern classrooms and computer labs
  • Strong emphasis on practical, hands-on learning
  • Safe, inclusive, and supportive environment
  • Affordable quality education for the Byumba community
